About Me

I’m an Electrical Engineering student who enjoys building and working on real-world projects. I’ve had hands-on experience with things like embedded systems, power systems, PCB design, control systems, and software development, and I like being able to turn ideas into something practical.

Over time, I’ve built a solid understanding of circuit simulation and power electronics, and I’m comfortable taking projects from early concepts through to testing and manufacturing.
