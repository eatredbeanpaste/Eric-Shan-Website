Battery Level Monitoring System

Kicad, PCB Fabrication, Soldering

电池电量监测系统

Kicad, PCB 制造, 焊接

Designed and implemented a 12V lithium battery level monitoring system with visual LED indicators and an emergency relay cutoff to prevent damage from low-voltage conditions. Managed the complete PCB development lifecycle, including schematic capture, circuit simulation, component selection, PCB layout optimization for signal integrity, fabrication, soldering, and full assembly.

设计并实现了一个带 LED 指示灯和紧急继电器切断功能的 12V 锂电池电量监测系统，以防止低压损坏。管理了完整的 PCB 开发生命周期，包括原理图设计、电路仿真、元器件选型、旨在保证信号完整性的 PCB 布局优化、制造、焊接和整机组装。

DC - DC Flyback Transformer

Altium Designer, PCB Fabrication, Soldering

DC - DC 反激式变压器

Altium Designer, PCB 制造, 焊接

Designed and validated a current-mode controlled flyback converter using the UC3843 for isolated DC–DC conversion. Performed key design calculations for switching parameters, transformer (turns ratio, inductance, core selection), and compensation to ensure stability. Integrated protection circuits including RCD snubber and implemented optocoupler-based feedback.

基于 UC3843 设计并验证了电流模式控制的反激式转换器，用于隔离式 DC-DC 转换。进行了开关参数、变压器（匝数比、电感、磁芯选择）和补偿电路的关键设计计算以确保稳定性。集成了包括 RCD 吸收电路在内的保护电路，并实现了基于光耦的反馈调节。
